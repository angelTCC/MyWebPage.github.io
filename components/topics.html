Ing. Agriculture

We are facing challenging times, with energy and food security being some of the key issues. Agricultural engineering not only addresses food-related problems but also plays a crucial role in projects related to energy, hydrology, agroindustry, and the environment. By applying agricultural concepts, we can improve the application and understanding of AI models and remote sensing, especially since agriculture is often focused on fieldwork, making it an ideal complement to computer-based analysis. Examples of this include contrasting soil and water components using remote sensing, interpreting the results of models applied to agriculture, monitoring fields with satellite imagery, studying land to develop energy projects like solar and wind, and more.
